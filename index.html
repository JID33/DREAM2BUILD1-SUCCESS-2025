How the Rotating Dollar System Works

Each day, 10 or more members contribute $10. One person receives the full amount minus 1/3 and 10% reserve. The remaining rotates to the next day. This builds a rotating cooperative wealth model. Payments can be via Cash, Mastercard, PayPal, MonCash, or NatCash.
